## Bounties / tracks — all three (each **Winner $3,000**)
⚠️ **Currency conflict:** the platform shows **$3,000** each (**$9,000** total). Notion + photos said **£3,000 / £9,000**. Confirm GBP vs USD on the day.
**Track ↔ bounty mapping now confirmed** (resolves the earlier "Bounty 01/03" gap):

### 🏆 Bounty 01 — The Small Business Productivity Powerhouse ($3,000)
Automate a real, painful workflow that is **reliable + accurate**, **easy to adopt for non-technical users**, and **clearly time-saving**. **Xero must be central to the workflow, not just an add-on.**
- **Build examples:** auto-categorising receipts/expenses/transactions · smart invoice reconciliation/matching · predicting stock shortages from sales trends · automating payroll/contractor payments · end-to-end (receipt → expense → reconciliation → reporting).
- **Strong submissions** go beyond simple rules — use AI to handle edge cases, messy data, real-world variability.

### 🔗 Bounty 02 — The Vibe Integrator ($3,000)
Intelligent, AI-powered integrations that **adapt and "understand" data across systems** (vs rigid if-this-then-that that breaks on real data).
- Projects: connect Xero with another platform (CRM, e-commerce, payroll, SaaS) · use an agent/AI layer to **interpret, map, and sync data dynamically** · **handle messy/inconsistent/incomplete data gracefully**. Focus = flexibility + intelligence, not just connectivity.
- **Build examples:** CRM deals → Xero invoices with smart field mapping · Shopify/Stripe-style data → Xero with AI categorisation · monitor another app + auto-trigger Xero actions · a "universal translator" for business data between tools.
- **Winning** = AI replaces brittle integration logic with adaptive, context-aware syncing.

### 💸 Bounty 03 — The Cash Flow Accelerator ($3,000)
Help businesses **grow** money, not just track it. Build an app/agent that **actively identifies and acts on revenue opportunities** using Xero data.
- Projects: analyse Xero accounting/payments data · surface meaningful, actionable insights · take **proactive steps** to increase revenue / improve cash flow.
- **Build examples:** detect lapsed high-value customers + trigger outreach · upsell/cross-sell opportunities · recommend subscription conversions for repeat purchases · predict late payments + automate follow-ups · highlight underperforming products/services.
- **Best** = data analysis **+ autonomous action** → measurable business outcomes.
